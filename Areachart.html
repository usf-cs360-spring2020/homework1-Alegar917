D3 and Tableau Area Chart

Data acquired from DataSF on February 9, 2020.

Analysis

For the area chart, the data was wrangled by using the SF data filer to lower the dataset to only 2019. The X-axis is a formation of the Activity Period to be monthly representation and Y-axis is the sum of Passenger Count. To interpret the color of the area, the blue means it was a domestic flight and orange means it was an international flight. To interpret the data, the value of domestic flight means it is size difference compared to the international area, it does not mean the domestic flight is 4 million but rather Domestic flight is 3 million and international was 1 million in the month of January for an example. This chart allows a direct comparsion of domestic and international flight in SF, which helped illustrate that Domestic flight had the majority passenger amount.
